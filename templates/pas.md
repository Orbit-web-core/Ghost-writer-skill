# PAS Template

## Problem
[State the problem clearly. One sentence. Make it specific.]

## Agitate
[Make it hurt. Show the cost. Who's losing money? Who's losing time? What's the invisible cost of not fixing this?]

## Solve
[Present the solution. Be specific. Not "hire an agency" — "build a revenue system that Google finds and AI recommends." Include 1-2 concrete steps.]

---

**Example:**

**Problem:** Most service businesses have websites that don't generate leads.

**Agitate:** You're paying for a digital brochure while your competitors get the calls you should be getting. Every month you wait, they pull further ahead.

**Solve:** Build a revenue system, not a website. Start with technically perfect pages that Google can find and AI can recommend. Then optimize for conversion, not aesthetics.
