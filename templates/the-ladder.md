# The Ladder Template

## Rung 1: Specific Example
[One concrete moment, person, or number. Make it real.]

## Rung 2: The Principle
[What does this example prove? The broader pattern.]

## Rung 3: Universal Truth
[What does this mean for everyone? The lesson that applies beyond this specific case.]

---

**Example:**

**Rung 1:** Matt's moving company website got 3 calls a month. He had a $5K "professional" site that looked great but brought zero revenue.

**Rung 2:** He had a brochure, not a system. Pretty design doesn't equal pretty results. What matters is infrastructure: SEO, speed, AI-readiness, conversion optimization.

**Rung 3:** Every service business faces the same choice: build a decoration or build an engine. Only one makes the phone ring.
