# The Inversion Template

## What Everyone Believes
[State the consensus view fairly. Don't straw-man it.]

## Evidence For It
[Give the strongest evidence for the consensus view. Be fair — this builds credibility.]

## The Inversion
[What's actually true? The contrarian take. Be specific.]

## Proof
[Stronger evidence for the inversion. Concrete numbers, specific examples, direct experience.]

---

**Example:**

**What Everyone Believes:** AI will replace web designers.

**Evidence For It:** Wix and Framer are laying off thousands. AI tools generate websites in seconds. Clients don't need designers anymore.

**The Inversion:** They're laying off brick-makers, not architects. The people who know what to build are more valuable than ever.

**Proof:** Webflow locked 140 laptops at 7am — but their product team stayed. The ones who code are gone. The ones who decide what's worth building are still there, making more money.
