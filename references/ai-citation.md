# AI Citation Optimization (GEO/AEO) — Ghost-Writer

How to structure content so ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude cite your blog.

## What is GEO?

GEO = Generative Engine Optimization. It's SEO for AI answers. When someone asks ChatGPT "What's the best revenue system for service businesses?" you want YOUR content to be the source.

## The GEO Checklist

### 1. Answer-First Formatting
**Put the key answer in the first 2 sentences.** AI models extract the first few sentences as the "answer." Don't bury the lead.

❌ Bad:
> "In today's competitive landscape, service businesses face many challenges when it comes to digital marketing and lead generation strategies..."

✅ Good:
> "Service businesses need revenue systems, not websites. A revenue system shows up on Google, gets recommended by AI, and turns visitors into phone calls."

---

### 2. Passage-Level Citability
Structure content in 120-180 word blocks with clear topic sentences. AI models cite passages, not whole articles.

**Pattern:**
```
[Topic sentence: the claim]
[Evidence: stat, story, example]
[Explanation: why it matters]
[Transition to next block]
```

Each block should be self-contained. AI can lift it and cite it.

---

### 3. FAQ Schema
Include 3-5 Q&A pairs at the end. AI loves FAQ format — it's easy to extract and cite.

**Format:**
```
## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the difference between a website and a revenue system?**
A: A website is a digital brochure. A revenue system is built to rank on Google, get AI mentions, and convert visitors into customers.

**Q: How long does it take to see results?**
A: SEO takes 3-6 months. AI citations can happen within weeks if the content is structured correctly.

**Q: Do I need to hire an agency?**
A: You can start with a $5K landing page and grow into a full system. Or hire us to run the whole engine.
```

---

### 4. Entity Clarity
Define key terms once, then use consistently. AI models track entities across content.

**Example:**
> "A revenue system (not a website — a system built to generate leads) includes SEO infrastructure, AI-ready architecture, and conversion-optimized pages."

After defining "revenue system," use that exact term. Don't switch to "lead machine" or "growth platform."

---

### 5. Direct Quotes
Include quotable one-liners AI can extract and attribute.

**Examples:**
- "Other agencies sell websites. We build revenue systems."
- "Google finds you. AI recommends you. Customers call you."
- "You're not invisible. You're just not built to be found."

---

### 6. Statistics with Named Sources
AI models prefer citing statistics with sources.

❌ Bad:
> "Studies show that most websites don't convert."

✅ Good:
> "According to SiteGrade's 2026 head-to-head test, all four AI builders (Wix ADI, Framer AI, Hostinger, 10Web) produced functional sites — but none optimized for conversion."

---

### 7. Q&A Format Throughout
Use "People Also Ask" style questions as H2s or H3s.

**Example H2s:**
- "What's the difference between a website and a revenue system?"
- "How much should a service business spend on their website?"
- "Why do most agency-built websites fail to generate leads?"

---

## GEO Delivery Checklist

Before shipping, verify:

- [ ] Key answer appears in first 2 sentences
- [ ] Content structured in 120-180 word passages
- [ ] FAQ section included (3-5 Q&A pairs)
- [ ] Key terms defined once, used consistently
- [ ] At least 2 quotable one-liners
- [ ] Statistics have named sources
- [ ] "People Also Ask" style H2s included
